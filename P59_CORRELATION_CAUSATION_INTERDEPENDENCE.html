CORRELATION_CAUSATION_INTERDEPENDENCE
CORRELATION: the observation of two separate events occurring within one finite interval of space and time such that those events appear to have no effect on each other's manifestation. CAUSATION: the observation of two separate events occurring within one finite interval of space and time such that one of those events (i.e. the oldest event) caused the other event (i.e. the newest event) to manifest such that the newest event would presumably would have not manifested unless the older event manifested exactly as it did.
