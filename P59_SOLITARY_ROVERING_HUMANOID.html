Bare in mind that I am speaking of imaginary objects which I willingly hallucinate for amusement. I would never want to intentionally harm some sentient creature.

ANTI_SOCIAL !== ASOCIAL

My super power is my ability to not give other embodied humanoids my affirmation.

That is why I have more time to think about the universe than most of those partialities.

ANTI_SOCIAL: the belief which some humanoid hosts such that the belief consists of the following message: humanoids (and other sufficiently sentient entities) are parasites.

ASOCIAL: the belief which some humanoid hosts such that the belief consists of the following message: humanoids and other sufficiently sentient creatures suffice for my continued level of happiness.

SOCIAL: I must pretend I am one of the individual members of the dominant species.
