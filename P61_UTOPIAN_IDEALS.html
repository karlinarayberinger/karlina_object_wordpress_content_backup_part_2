UTOPIAN_IDEALS
First of all, ensure that there exist a sufficient number of photovoltaic cells which work properly and which are contributing a sufficiently high daily electricity output before closing down all polluting types of electricity generating facilities. Secondly, require that all cars in service include a self-driving override feature which takes over the acceleration and steering controls of the vehicle if an accident is anticipated by the vehicle's built-in safety mechanisms (and that all vehicles in service are electrically powered rather than petroleum combusion powered).
