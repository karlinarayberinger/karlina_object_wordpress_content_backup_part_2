LONG_TERM_PERSONAL_PLANNING
As I learn to adapt to living outside full time while subsisting on as little money as possible (but not at the expense of what I think is a minimally satisfying quality of life), I find it impossible to be nocturnal as I used to while living with more accommodations (and more supervision). It is easy for me to sleep through the night and wake up naturally when the sunrises. I am doing my best to not settle for a kind of job and lifestyle which makes me feel like a traitor to my own cause. I have no idea if or when I will get a job, but I think it is reasonable to expect that I will have a job by the end of the year. I am grateful for the bounties I happen to acquire and I do get some real enjoyment out of urban camping as a game of survival (and, perhaps contradictory to some, keeping myself as high-tech, modern, and well-educated as possible (rather than devolving into the conformist stereotype of a knuckle-dragging Neanderthal). Planet Earth and human civilization appear to be undergoing significant crisis and change. As an individual humanoid, I am doing my best to help human civilization flourish and evolve.
