Just nonchalant figures swaggering listlessly in haughty tepid dreams. Make a mockery of the now and zen. Keep thoughts placid as sedated pigs in a pen. Children are token chattel which are raised on tortured cattle. They are raised to treat all animals as mere chattel. So I hear the jail cage rattle.
I wish that the entire (human) world would adopt my sensibilities as a vegan, environmentally conscious, holistic health valuing, trans-speciest sentient, egalitarian, nonviolent communication promoting, censorship adverse, human-machine-interface evolution enabling, scientifically inquisitive, genuinely open minded, conscious expanding, and individuality promoting species overnight and wake up to restart human civilization on much more secure and joyous grounding. Even though such a wish is quite idealistic and unlikely to manifest, expressing that wish here makes it more likely to come true (at least in increments).
I apologize for offending anyone, but my achy breaky heart yearns to say this: I realize that the human world around me is driven by the pain of slaughtered animals. I can feel it in my body (now that I know the difference between eating meat and adhering to a nutritionally BALANCED plant-based diet for more than one week): on plants only, I feel more light on my feet and sensitive to the environment. By contrast, after eating meat, I sort of feel like a bouncer in a world overcrowded with pissed off bouncers impatient to get ahead of whatever imagined competition exists. I see people unapologetically polluting the air, water, and soil with loud gas guzzling automobiles which vaguely resemble steroid ridden military-grade tanks (which makes a simple drive to the grocery store look like a cut-throat military operation). Here is the real kicker (pun intended)... I think veganism is not even half as popular as it would be for one major factor: the fact that society bullies women into getting pregnant and, as a consequence, women are practically force-fed animal products so that Baby gets all the nutrients it needs while Big Bear Pappa stands by and ensures that His fattening sow and her litter of spawn come out of the oven as robust and red-blooded American as possible. I am trying to gross women out who have yet to "fall pregnant" so that they are not turned into victims of the slaughtering industry (and cut off from the holistic, naturalistic sensibilities which would otherwise prevail if they did not have to be brainwashed and hobbled into becoming factory farmed animals themselves). I really have a vendetta against the unnecessarily high rate of pregnancy on Planet Earth at this time. I wish more people would devote themselves to helping to fix the ecosystem and societal infrastructure before selling out as birthing factories for children whose futures are treated as unimportant.
